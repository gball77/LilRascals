About Us

Lil Rascal LLC is an in-home community daycare program and educational after-school program that supports the needs of disadvantaged families throughout Stockton, CA. The center targets low-income families and children with special needs. Lil Rascal LLC is working to address the challenges of at-risk youth affected by Stockton's prevalent crime and violence rates, homelessness, and substance abuse. The daycare program is dedicated to providing educational programs and career development opportunities.

Lil Rascal LLC is a groundbreaking, community-driven educational program that is dedicated to serving the educational and career development needs of at-risk youth. Because Stockton is one of the nation's largest poverty-stricken communities, many families and children are greatly impacted by the city's increasing crime and violence rate, and homelessness challenges. Lil Rascal LLC has created an educational program designed to break the stigmas that affect marginalized communities by providing empowerment, education, and career development opportunities for the city's at-risk youth

Services We Offer

  • safe and nurturing daycare program
  • after-school programs
  • before and after-school drop off and pickup service
  • technology-based learning programs (laptops, desktops, and mobile tablet devices)
  • on-site tutors
  • workshop programs for educational learning and discovery
  • outdoor exploratory learning
  • daily healthy meals, a wide variety of snacks, and beverages
