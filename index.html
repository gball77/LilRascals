About Us

Founded in 2002, Lil Rascal LLC is a community daycare and learning-based after-school program that supports the needs of disadvantaged families throughout Stockton, CA.

The program proudly serves low-income families and children with special needs, and is working to address the challenges of at-risk youth affected by Stockton's prevalent crime and violence rates, homelessness, and substance abuse

As a groundbreaking, community-driven educational program that is dedicated to serving the educational and career development needs of at-risk youth, Lil Rascal LLC stands at the intersection of education and empowerment for Stocktonâ€™s underserved youth

Because Stockton is one of the nation's largest poverty-stricken communities, many families and children are greatly impacted by the city's increasing crime and violence rate, and homelessness challenges. Lil Rascal LLC has created an educational daycare and after-school program designed to break the stigmas that affect marginalized communities by providing empowerment, education, and career development opportunities for the city's at-risk youth.
